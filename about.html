Our Story

Always Close to Your Heart

Forever Part of Me Jewellery was created to help families keep cherished memories close. Every piece is handmade using silver clay, resin, acrylic, and natural horsehair.

With a strong equestrian influence, we specialise in horsehair bracelets, pawprint jewellery, ash‑infused silver, and bespoke sculpted keepsakes.
