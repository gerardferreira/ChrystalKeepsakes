Our Story

Chrystal Keepsakes was born from a love of animals, artistry and the desire to help families keep cherished memories close.

Our studio specialises in equestrian-inspired artwork including horsehair bracelets, personalised sculptures, pawprint pendants and ash‑infused jewellery.
