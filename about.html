Our Story

Chrystal Keepsakes was born from a love of animals, artistry and the desire to help families keep cherished memories close. Every piece is handmade with care, using materials such as silver clay, resin, acrylic and natural horsehair.

With a strong equestrian influence, we specialise in horsehair bracelets, pawprint jewellery, ash‑infused silver, and bespoke sculpted keepsakes.
